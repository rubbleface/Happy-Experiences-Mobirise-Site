O.A.T. Club (Coming Soon!)

The LJB Outdoors, Adventure and Travel Club (O.A.T.) is for those who are game to spend time outdoors.  

You don't need to be an athlete, scout or seasoned explorer to come onboard.

We're just a bunch of happy folk who enjoy traversing or exploring both concrete jungles and lush foliage, both in our backyard and on foreign shores .


Before this product is officially launched, we're looking for a core group of people who Enjoy Nature and Participating in          Group Activities!



The exciting bit:


As an Early Bird, you'll enjoy benefits such as:

  1. Access to Early Bird or Exclusive Content
  2. Opportunities to Suggest Activities
  3. Discounts to Events and Activities


The boring but important bit:  


By leaving your email address, you agree to:

  1. Be contacted by LJB so we can get to know you better :)
  2. Be added to a Whataspp or email discussion group
  3. Receive updates from time to time
