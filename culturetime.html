Culture Time

A nourishing space for those who
=> desire to understand history, society and their place in it
=> get in touch with the arts
=> enjoy learning, exploring, experiencing and discussing 
=> are interested in ideas, customs, themes, genres and behaviours

Music Appreciation Course

"If music be the food of love, play on".
Come spend time listening to and discussing diverse music genres, learn basic rhythm and note-reading, or even play a simple percussion or melodic instrument. 

More

Pop Culture Course

"Pop culture is a reflection of social change"
Come examine the attitudes, ideas, images, perspectives (and other phenomena) within the mainstream Western, Asian and global cultures of the 20th and 21st century.

More

Theatre Appreciation Course

"Drama is life with the dull bits cut out".
Come learn more about drama--onstage, backstage, behind-the-scenes, you name it. A brief history of theatre and a trip to catch some performances  will be included.
