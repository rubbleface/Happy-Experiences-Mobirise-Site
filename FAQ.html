F.A.Q. Time!

Q: How did you come up with the name of your company?

A: Thanks for asking! It's actually the #1 question we get.

The name's been around for more than twenty years. Our first names begin with L, J and B respectively. As kids, we used LJB as the name of our organisation whenever we role-played art teachers, bus inspectors, restaurant chefs, company heads and swimming coaches.

It's nice to be able to turn LJB into a registered reality.
