Club "More than Books"

LJB's "More than Books" Reading Club is a warm and inviting space for those who like thinking and talking about books, to think and talk about books. Join us for book recommendations, book reviews, facilitated discussions and literature-related activities.

Expect lively and thought-provoking views and ideas each time we gather! 


Before this product is officially launched, we'd like to invite you to join a Core Group who love reading, and who wish to spread that love.

  • Access to Early Bird or Exclusive Content
  • Opportunities to Suggest Reading Activities
  • Discounts to Events and Activities
