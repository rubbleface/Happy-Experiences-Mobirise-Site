Club "We Love Hamsters"

Whether a beloved first pet, a cautious first encounter or a lifelong obsession, these super cute and plush critters have become an important part of many a Happy Experience.

Watch them play, watch them eat, watch them groom, and pick up tips about how to create the perfect home environment for your furry friend. Our focus is on ethical breeding, adoption and rearing.

Beginners, Experts, Humans and Hamsters alike warmly welcomed!
