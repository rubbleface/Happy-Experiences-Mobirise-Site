Wellness Clinics (coming soon!)

At LJB, wellness refers to healing and maintenance that considers the whole person---body, mind, spirit, emotions -- in the quest for optimal health and wellness. 

We currently don't offer professional counselling, psychiatric, medical or fitness advice.

But we do draw on ancient wisdom, common sense, readily-available yet credible research and empathy to craft programmes that help committed participants begin a life-long journey towards personal satisfaction and inner well-being. 

Before this product is officially launched, we're looking for a core group of people whose PASSION IS WELLNESS to join us as Early Birds.


The exciting bit:

As an Early Bird, you'll enjoy benefits such as:
  1. Access to Early Bird or Exclusive Content
  2. Opportunities to Suggest Activities at Focus Group sessions
  3. Discounts to Events and Activities

The important bit:
