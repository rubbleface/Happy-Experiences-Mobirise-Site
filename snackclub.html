Wait...a legit Snack Club?!

We're all for a healthy lifestyle...you know, eating junk food in moderation, exercising regularly, reducing stress levels etc. We believe in and practise that. 

At the same time however, we are also unabashed snackheads, and snacks really do make us happy. Japanese rice crackers, Kettle's potato chips, cake or biscuits in foreign-made packages, you name it, we'd like to try it, rate it and share it. 

We realise snacks are best eaten with good company, and especially enjoyable when many flavours can be sampled at once. That's why we need you to come along and join our snack party.

The current idea is to collect a participation fee that includes unlimited access to an afternoon's snacks, maybe a free movie or boardgame and a nice selection of affordable thirst quenchers and yummy desserts. Sounds good?
