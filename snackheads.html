Wait...you're celebrating Snacking?!

We're all for a healthy lifestyle...you know, eating junk food in moderation, exercising regularly, reducing stress levels etc.

At the same time however, we are also unabashed snackheads, and snacks really do make us happy. Japanese rice crackers, potato chips with exotic flavour combinations, cake and biscuits from every continent...you name it, we'd like to try it, rate it and share it!

Snacks are best eaten with company, and especially enjoyable when conversation is flowing. That's why we need you to come along and join our snack party.

The current idea is to collect a participation fee that includes unlimited access to a tableful of snacks, with a movie or boardgame thrown in, plus a nice selection of affordable thirst quenchers and desserts. Sounds good?
