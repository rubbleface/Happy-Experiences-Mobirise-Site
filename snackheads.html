Wait...you're celebrating Snacking?!

We're all for a balanced lifestyle...eating junk food in moderation, exercising regularly, reducing stress levels....

At the same time however, we're unabashed snackheads. Snacks REALLY do make us happy. Japanese rice crackers, potato chips in exotic flavour combinations, cakes and cookies from every continent...you name it, let's try, rate and share it!

We also think some snacks are best eaten with company, when conversation is flowing. That's why we'd like you to come along and join our snack parties.
