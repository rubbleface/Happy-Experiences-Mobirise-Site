Film Events

Our goal is to encourage people living in Singapore to expand their film-viewing repertoire.

Whether indie flicks, cult classics, world cinema or blockbusters; well-made and well-intentioned films deserve a wider audience.

Want to learn more about films, and to understand yourself and the world better?

Come join our screenings, excursions and online discussions!

QUESTION: What happens after I make payment?

STEP 1. You will receive a follow-up email with a virtual ticket. 

STEP 2. Check available screenings on the Film Club Blog and use the serial number on the ticket to secure a seat at the screening of your choice.

Let us know your choice of complimentary beverage.

STEP 3. Email, SMS or Whatsapp your preferences to LJB.

STEP 4. You will receive a confirmation email or text subject to availability.

STEP 5. See you at the screening!
